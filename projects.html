Gut Life


Database for relationships between gut bacteria and diseases in humans using NLP.

Data extraction carried out using Natural Language Processing from PubMed and PMC abstracts using.

Website built using Django and PostGreSQL available at gutlife.net

Information available - diseases, bacteria taxonomy and sample type information.

Unique Repeats in Bacterial Proteome


Identification of unique sequences composed of unique subsequences

Targeted nearly 60 bacterial proteomes. Included only those bacteria that cause diseases in humans.

Identification of characteristically unique sequences were done such that no repeats of the said sequences or the subsequences of these sequences were present anywhere in the genomes across all analysed bacterias.

Technology - Python. Can be accessed here.
